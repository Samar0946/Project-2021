His Story

Vincent Van Gogh was a Dutch post-impressionist painter who is among the most famous and influential figures in the history of Western art.

He born in 1853, and died in 1890 by commiting a suicide. In his last days he started crying continously then he shoot him self in sunfloor field where he used to paint.

One of the most famous paint for him is "Stary Night" which consider as an icon in the post-impression art, Also he had some other paints like: Blooming Flowers, BedRoom in Arles, and The Night Cafe.

He loved his doctor's daughter but they weren't meant to be with each others. He died in a very early age, poor, lonely, and sad. After That He became on of the masters of the post-impression art in his time.
